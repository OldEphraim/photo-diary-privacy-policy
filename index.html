1. Introduction

Photo Diary is a journaling app that allows users to capture daily moments through photos, videos, and optional captions. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Photo Diary application, available on iOS devices through the Apple App Store.

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data.

By downloading, accessing, or using Photo Diary, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you use Photo Diary, you may provide the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address or other authentication information through our authentication provider (Clerk).
  • User Content: The photos, videos, text, and other content you upload to the app as part of your journal entries.
  • Communication: If you contact us directly, we may collect additional information you provide, such as your name, email address, and the contents of your message.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use Photo Diary, certain information may be collected automatically, including:

  • Device Information: Information about your mobile device, including device model, operating system and version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Information: Information about how you use the app, such as the features you use, the time and duration of your activities, and other usage patterns.
  • Log Information: Error reports, API usage, and other app analytics that help us improve the application.

2.3 Information We Do Not Collect

Photo Diary does not:

  • Collect precise GPS location data (unless you explicitly grant permission)
  • Access your contacts
  • Track your browsing activity outside of the app
  • Collect payment information directly (any payments are processed through Apple's App Store)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Provide and Maintain the Service: To deliver the functionality of Photo Diary, including storing and displaying your journal entries.
  • Improve and Develop: To understand how users interact with our app, identify and fix issues, and develop new features.
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries, provide support, and send service-related communications.
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, security breaches, or potentially prohibited or illegal activities.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or enforceable governmental requests.

4. Data Storage and Security

All user data, including photos, videos, and text entries, is stored securely on Amazon Web Services (AWS) S3 using encrypted connections. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS for all data transfers
  • Secure authentication through Clerk
  • Encryption of data in transit
  • Access controls to limit data access to authorized personnel only

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. However, we may share information with trusted third parties who assist us in operating our app, as long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential. These trusted third parties include:

  • Clerk: Our authentication provider, which handles user authentication. Please refer to Clerk's Privacy Policy for more information.
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS): Our cloud storage provider, where your content is securely stored. Please refer to AWS's Privacy Policy for more information.
  • Analytics Providers: We may use analytics providers to help us understand app usage patterns. Any data shared for analytics purposes is aggregated and de-identified.

We may also disclose your information:

  • If required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, legal proceedings, or similar legal process.
  • When we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request.
  • If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, you will be notified via email and/or a prominent notice in our app of any change in ownership or uses of your personal information.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control of Your Information

You can access, view, and delete the content you've created within the Photo Diary app at any time. You control your journal entries and can remove them when you choose.

6.2 Account Information

You can review and update your account information through the app settings. If you wish to delete your account, you can do so through the app or by contacting us directly.

6.3 Data Deletion

If you delete your account, we will delete all of your content from our active systems. However, some information may remain in our backups for a limited period before being permanently deleted.

6.4 Do Not Track

Photo Diary does not track users across third-party websites or services, so Do Not Track (DNT) signals from web browsers are not applicable to our app. However, we are committed to providing you with meaningful choices about the information we collect.

6.5 Communication Preferences

If you have opted to receive communications from us, you can opt out of receiving further communications by contacting us at a8garber@yahoo.com.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the services of Photo Diary. We will delete your personal information upon account deletion, except where we need to retain certain information for legitimate business or legal purposes.

Examples of information we may retain even after account deletion include:

  • Information necessary for legal compliance or regulatory requirements
  • Information to resolve disputes or enforce our agreements
  • Aggregated, de-identified data that does not identify any individual user

8. Children's Privacy

Photo Diary is not intended for users under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information from our servers immediately. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.

9. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have certain rights regarding your personal information under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and other California privacy laws. These rights include:

  • The right to know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information
  • The right to opt-out of the sale of your personal information (note that we do not sell personal information)
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact Information section. We will respond to verifiable consumer requests within 45 days.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in countries other than the one in which you reside. By using Photo Diary, you consent to the transfer of information to countries outside your country of residence, including the United States, which may have data protection rules that are different from those of your country.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, we comply with applicable data protection laws when transferring your personal information outside of these areas.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page. Your continued use of Photo Diary after we post any modifications to the Privacy Policy will constitute your acknowledgment of the modifications and your consent to abide and be bound by the modified Privacy Policy.
